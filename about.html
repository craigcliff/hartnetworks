All You Want To Know About Us

Proudly 100% South African, owned and operated, Hartnetworks IT Solutions was founded by IT professionals Bjorn Hartnick and Craig Hector, who collectively have more than 20 years individual experience, in the IT industry. Other than custom-fit IT Services, our big focus is our Clients. Knowing our clients meticulously is how we gear IT Solutions that are more than just an ideal fit – we gear IT Solutions with the growth of your organisation in mind. This enables us and the system we built for you to grow with you. Whether you need Hartnetwork IT Solutions for applications support, hardware/software support, or website design/hosting, we are able to handle your needs, all of them, whatever they may be. If you need a custom infrastructure designed, built and managed from the ground up, then we are your team! With Hartnetwork IT Solutions, you do not need to shop elsewhere for anything at all – we take care of everything for you. Hartnetwork IT Solutions is your one-stop shop for all IT solutions, Systems, Service and Support. Our goal is to take care of all IT infrastructure and IT related needs so that our clients are free to focus on their business growing - their success is our success as well!
